Greek Zaziki Recipe

Ingredients List

  • 2 cups grated cucumber
  • 1 Â½ cups plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint and/or dill
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 medium clove garlic, pressed or minced

Steps

  1. Lightly squeeze the grated cucumber between your palms
  2. Transfer the squeezed cucumber to a serving bowl
  3. Add the yogurt, olive oil, herbs, lemon juice, garlic, and salt to the bowl, and stir to blend
  4. Serve tzatziki immediately or chill for later
  5. Enjoy!
