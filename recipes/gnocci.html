Grandma's Gnocci Recipe

Ingredients List

  • 4 medium russet potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt, plus more for the water
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Â½ cups all-purpose flour (190 g), extra to dust
  • 2 tablespoons butter, for pan frying

Steps

  1. Put potatoes water on to boil
  2. Remove the skin of potatoes
  3. Put 1 cups of flour onto a clean surface and turn out the potato dough onto it
  4. If desired, place a fork on your work surface and slide each gnocchi
  5. In a pan over medium heat, melt butter
  6. Add the gnocchi and toss until lightly golden
  7. Enjoy!
